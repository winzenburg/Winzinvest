Market Regime Engine

Before any strategy runs each day, the system classifies the market using SPY vs its 200-day SMA and VIX. Regime controls allocation, which options strategies are active, and how aggressively capital is deployed.

  • STRONG_UPTREND 100% longs. Iron condors active when IV rank > 0.30. No shorts.
  • CHOPPY 92% longs, 8% shorts. Iron condors active on SPY & QQQ. CSPs eligible.
  • MIXED 90% longs, 10% shorts. Iron condors + protective puts active. CSPs eligible.
  • STRONG_DOWNTREND 50% longs only. No new CSPs (assignment risk). Protective puts active.
  • UNFAVORABLE No new positions. All options entry paused. Existing positions monitored only.

Trading Strategies

The system runs two layers: a hybrid equity engine and an options income overlay. Together they generated complementary returns — momentum equity in trending markets, iron condors in choppy/sideways markets.

Equity Layer

📈 Hybrid Momentum Longs

Core strategy. Hybrid NX + AMS scoring — composite trend, relative strength vs SPY, structure quality, HTF bias, and AMS volume score. Only candidates with combined score ≥ 0.50 are eligible.

TP: 3.5× ATR  |  Trail: 3.0× ATR (activates at 1R profit)  |  Stop: 1.5× ATR  |  Time: 20 days
Score floor 0.50 eliminates low-conviction setups
📉 Momentum Shorts (Reduced Hedge)

Small hedge overlay capped at 8–10% of slots. Entry requires composite < 0.20, structure < 0.20. Blocked entirely in uptrend, downtrend, and unfavorable regimes.

CHOPPY: 8% slots  |  MIXED: 10% slots  |  Others: blocked
🔄 Mean Reversion (RSI-2)

Buys oversold pullbacks (RSI(2) < 10) in stocks above 200 SMA. Quick in-and-out targeting bounce. Exits when RSI(2) recovers above 70.

Stop: 1.0× ATR  |  TP: 1.5× ATR  |  Max hold: 5 days
⚖️ Pairs Trading

Market-neutral long/short within correlated pairs. Enters when spread z-score > 2.0. Exits on mean reversion or time stop.

Stop: 2× ATR per leg  |  TP: 3× ATR  |  Exit: z-score < 0.5 or 20 days

Options Income Overlay

🦅 Iron Condors

Primary income driver. Sell 10% OTM put + 10% OTM call on SPY and QQQ. Buy 15% OTM wings. Credit ~30% of max risk. Both SPY and QQQ can open the same day. Max 4 open.

Active: CHOPPY, MIXED, STRONG_UPTREND (IV rank > 0.30)  |  DTE: 35
$32,348 · 36 trades · 100% win rate (2Y)
💰 Cash-Secured Puts

Sell puts below 50-EMA on stocks we want to own. Pullback 2.5–10%, near support (within 3%), IV rank ≥ 0.45. Early close if underlying drops 5%+ below strike to cap assignment losses.

Active: CHOPPY, MIXED, STRONG_UPTREND only  |  Universe: 57 names  |  DTE: 35
📞 Covered Calls

Sell calls against positions of ≥ 100 shares that are at least 1% above entry. Strike 6%+ OTM. IV rank ≥ 0.45. Delta targeted at ~0.20. Earnings blackout enforced.

DTE: 35  |  More impactful at $250K+ (need 100+ shares)
🛡️ Protective Puts

Buy SPY puts 7% OTM as portfolio insurance during uncertain regimes. Monthly budget scales with account — min($15,000, 0.75% of NLV). Max 2 open.

Active: MIXED, UNFAVORABLE, STRONG_DOWNTREND  |  DTE: 30  |  Insurance only

Execution Gates

Every order must pass all 8 gates before reaching IBKR. Any failure blocks the trade and logs to the audit trail.

  1. ✓Daily Loss Limit — daily realized + unrealized loss < 3% of equity
  2. ✓Sector Concentration — no single sector exceeds 30% of total notional
  3. ✓Gap Risk — no new orders within 60 min of market close
  4. ✓Regime Filter — blocks shorts in uptrends; halts all entries in unfavorable regime
  5. ✓Position Size — single position notional ≤ max % of equity
  6. ✓Total Notional Cap — gross notional ≤ 100% of equity
  7. ✓Portfolio Heat — total open risk (stops distance) ≤ 8% of equity
  8. ✓Earnings Blackout — no options within 7 days of earnings announcement
