# KINLET Phase 1 — Personalized DM Outreach Batch (Mar 2-3, 2026)

**Target:** 5+ high-intent prospects from new Reddit guides + Week 1 guide responses  
**Timeline:** Send Mar 2-3 (staggered, 2-3 per night)  
**Expected Response Rate:** 15-20% (based on Week 1 performance)  
**Expected Signups:** 4-8 from this batch  

---

## DM Strategy

**Targeting Approach:**
- Identify Reddit users from recent guide comments + upvoters
- Look for specific pain points in their comments
- Reference their specific comment (shows we read it, not template)
- Offer solution, not sales pitch
- CTA: "Building this resource, would love your feedback" or "Would you find this useful?"

---

## Personalized DM Batch (Mar 2-3)

### DM 1 — Recent Diagnosis Pain Point

**Target User:** Likely comment on "Recent Diagnosis - First 90 Days" guide (Mar 2)  
**Pain Point Identifier:** "Just got diagnosed," "feeling lost," "don't know where to start"  

**Sample DM:**

> Hey [Name], saw your comment on the recent diagnosis post about feeling overwhelmed. I get it—that first week is brutal.
>
> Quick backstory: I've spent years building tools for caregivers going through exactly what you're dealing with. The roadmap I shared (90-day breakdown) is what I wish someone had given me too.
>
> Curious: what part of the first 90 days feels most overwhelming right now? Is it the paperwork, the daily routine stuff, or just knowing what to do first?
>
> I'm building a resource specifically for this moment, and feedback from people like you is gold. Would you be open to answering a few quick questions? No sales, no pressure—just trying to understand what actually helps.
>
> Let me know.

**Success Metric:** Reply with specific pain point (enables product iteration)
