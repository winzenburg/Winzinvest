## RESPONSE MANAGEMENT WORKFLOW

**If Response is "Yes, let's grab coffee":**
1. Reply within 2 hours (show urgency)
2. Suggest 2-3 time slots (Calendly or direct)
3. Topic: "Curious about [company's] direction + how I might help"
4. Prepare: 2-3 thoughtful questions (not job interview questions)
5. Success: Schedule for mid-March (give time to build momentum)

**If Response is "Happy to chat via email":**
1. Follow up within 12 hours
2. Send 2-3 specific questions (not generic)
3. Keep email to <200 words (respect their time)
4. Offer coffee as next step

**If Response is "Not available, but here's [contact]":**
1. Reply to original contact: "Thanks for the intro, will reach out"
2. Message new contact within 24 hours
3. Reference original contact (warm intro chain)
4. Same positioning, tailored to new role

**If Response is "No response" (after 3 days):**
1. Wait 3 days from initial send
2. Send gentle follow-up (not pushy)
3. New angle: "Thought you might find this interesting..."
4. If 2nd message also no response: Move on to next person

**If Response is "Not interested":**
1. Reply: "Thanks for letting me know, good luck with [company]!"
2. Stay on their radar (you never know)
3. Move to next contact
